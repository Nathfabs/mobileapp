Spirited Away Dumplings
Ingredients
Dumpling wrappers
Ground pork
Vegetables
Garlic
Soy sauce
Sesame oil
Cooking Steps
1
In a bowl, combine about 1 cup of ground pork or chopped vegetables with 2 cloves of minced garlic.
2
Add 1 tablespoon soy sauce and 1 teaspoon sesame oil, then mix thoroughly until well combined.
3
Place 1 spoonful of filling in the center of each dumpling wrapper.
4
Lightly wet the edges with water, fold the wrapper, and press firmly to seal.
5
Arrange dumplings inside a steamer lined with parchment paper.
6
Steam over boiling water for 10–12 minutes until fully cooked.
7
Serve hot with soy sauce or dipping sauce.
