Totoro's Matcha Cloud
Ingredients
Matcha
Milk
Sugar
Whipped cream
Cooking Steps
1
Heat 1 cup of milk in a small saucepan or microwave until warm but not boiling.
2
In a cup, mix 1 teaspoon of matcha powder with 2 tablespoons of sugar.
3
Pour a small amount of warm milk first and stir well to dissolve the matcha smoothly.
4
Slowly add the remaining milk while mixing to create a creamy drink.
5
Top generously with whipped cream to form a soft “cloud” effect.
5
Serve warm and enjoy the cozy forest-inspired drink.
