Kiki’s Strawberry Cake
Ingredients
Sponge cake base
Cocoa powder
Melted chocolate
Butter
Sugar
Eggs
Flour
Whipped cream
Chocolate frosting
Strawberries
White chocolate
Cooking Steps
1
Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and prepare a round cake pan by lightly greasing it with butter and lining it with parchment paper.
2
In a mixing bowl, combine ½ cup softened butter and ¾ cup sugar, then mix until light and creamy.
3
Add 2 eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
4
Stir in ½ cup melted chocolate or cocoa mixture to give the cake its rich chocolate flavor.
5
Gradually fold in 1 cup flour and mix until the batter becomes smooth and thick.
6
Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and bake for 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
7
Allow the cake to cool completely before decorating.
8
Spread a smooth layer of chocolate frosting or whipped cream over the entire cake to create the dark surface seen in Kiki’s cake.
9
Melt white chocolate or prepare white icing, then carefully pipe or draw Kiki’s silhouette and lettering on top of the cake.
10
Decorate lightly with small strawberry accents if desired, then chill the cake for 20–30 minutes before serving.
